Cologne
=======

.. articleMetaData::
   :Where: Elten, Germany
   :Date: 20040306 0940 CET
   :Tags: 

Apparently they didn't invent automatic signals in Germany yet and
all the railway/road crossings where manually barricaded by people
in orange jackets and red/white colored banners. The official
reason was that the signs all fell out between Zevenaar and
Emmerich.
